First Project

My very first project was a mini scratch game called “run from the sharks” (2017). Here, the player controls a little fish with the ability to shoot witches at sharks (occasionally). Despite its many bugs, the game introduced me to loops, conditionals, and event handling. It ignited my passion for coding, ultimately leading me to pursue computer science.
